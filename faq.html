Common Questions

What Dealer Principals
Actually Ask Us.

Eight things every dealership wants to know before signing. Straight answers, no fluff.

Does Drive AI Sales work with my existing CRM like CDK, Reynolds, VinSolutions or DealerSocket?
Yes. We build custom integrations around your current CRM and phone system. We do not require you to switch platforms. We work alongside your existing tools so your team's workflow does not change, but leads stop falling through the cracks. We will scope the specific integration with you on the discovery call.
How fast can Drive AI Sales go live in my dealership?
Most dealerships are live within 24 hours of signing. Standard integrations with common CRMs, phone systems, inventory software, and booking platforms are pre-built. Bespoke integrations may take longer and we will give you a clear timeline on the discovery call.
Who owns the customer data Drive AI Sales processes?
The dealership owns 100 percent of its customer and lead data at all times. Drive AI Sales is the processor, not the owner. You can export your data at any time. We comply with PIPEDA, Alberta's PIPA, and other applicable Canadian privacy legislation. Full details in our Client Terms.
What happens if the AI says something wrong to a customer?
Every Drive AI deployment includes human-in-the-loop oversight. Your team can review, edit, or take over any AI conversation in real time. We tune the AI to your brand voice, your inventory, and your compliance constraints before going live. AMVIC and OMVIC advertising rules are configured into the system. If the AI is unsure, it escalates to a human.
What does Drive AI Sales cost?
Pricing depends on dealership size, lead volume, and depth of integration. Typical deployments pay for themselves with less than one additional sold deal per month. We give you a transparent quote during the 15-minute discovery call. There are no hidden fees, no per-seat charges, and no surprise renewal hikes.
Who pays for the Twilio phone and SMS charges?
The dealership pays for its own Twilio and messaging usage at cost. Drive AI does not mark up telephony or SMS charges. Expect roughly $50 to $200 per month in Twilio usage depending on your lead volume and call duration.
Can the AI handle bilingual leads in French, Punjabi, Mandarin or Spanish?
Yes. The underlying AI models support over 50 languages out of the box. We configure a dealer-specific bilingual or trilingual response policy based on your customer base. Common Alberta deployments include English, French, Punjabi, and Spanish.
What if my BDC team does not want to be replaced by AI?
Drive AI Sales is built to augment, not replace, your BDC team. The AI handles tier-1 response, qualification, and follow-up, freeing your team to focus on high-intent conversations and closing. Dealerships typically reassign BDC headcount to higher-leverage roles, not lay off. Want to walk through it for your team? Book a call.
